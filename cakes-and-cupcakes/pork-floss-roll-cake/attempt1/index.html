Recipe notes

Ended up better than expected? I tweaked the base cake recipe since I used less dry ingredients (no coffee), adding in less milk to compensate for the extra vanilla extract as well. I used less sugar since I tend to like my sweets with less sugar, which was probably correct? I think if I was craving more sweetness, I could use the original amount of sugar or just add more condensed milk. In addition, I used the recipe my mom had for mayo (per her request), which was 2 egg yolks, ~1 cup of oil, as well as some lime juice and salt.


The final result could use some work. It tasted pretty good, but I think we needed to use more pork and mayo in the cake to balance it out. I also need to figure out how to spread the mayo + pork without splitting the cake. The solution may be to spread the fillings before rolling up the cake to cool. We also ended up with a LOT of extra mayo, so maybe make less next time. Also, I think its ideal to leave it in the fridge overnight after you add the mixture so that it soaks into the cake.
