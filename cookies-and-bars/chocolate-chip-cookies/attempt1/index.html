Recipe notes

Refridgerated overnight and let thaw for over an hour. Cooked for 12 minutes. The texture was mostly soft (slightly fluffy?), with some crisp on the bottom/edges. I think I should cook for another minute. I also took the parchment off of the tray to cook other cookies faster, but leaving it on the tray might be the move. Also maybe slightly more chocolate? I ran out towards the end. I used a small cookie scooper, so this recipe yielded 25 cookies.
